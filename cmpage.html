General

City Morgue is a trap metal duo from New York City, USA. The band consists of three members:

  • Junius 'ZillaKami' Rogers, from Bay Shore
  • Vinicius 'SosMula' Sosa, from São Paulo
  • Producer Sami 'THRAXX' Nehari.
Their debut tape Vol. 1: Hell Or High Water released on October 12, 2018.

History

Growing up in Harlem, Sos took a lot of inspiration from the underground rap scene, listening to artists such as Eminem. Later on he met Peter 'Righteous P' Rogers, the co-founder of Hikari-Ultra, whose little brother was known as ZillaKami. Born and raised in Bay Shore, Long Island, Zilla was heavily involved in the Hardcore punk movement in his teens, but after being put onto the songs of DMX and Onyx and the Beastie Boys, he started mixing rap and metal sounds together. Eventually, ZillaKami and SosMula formed their band in 2017 and working with THRAXX, they dropped songs like "Yukk Mouth", "HOCKEY PUKK", "SHINNERS13" and "Drop Dead" on Soundcloud.

Music

The group's music has been categorised as punk rap, trap metal, hardcore rap, and nu metal. In fact, most City Morgue's songs contain elements of extreme metal and shock rock. Their biggest influences are Slipknot, Behemoth, Radiohead, DMX, Onyx, Beastie Boys, Eminem and Tyler, the Creator.
